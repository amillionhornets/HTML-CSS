My Top Video games

  1. Nier Automata
  2. Destiny 2
  3. Halo 3
  4. Tetris
  5. Legend of Zelda Breath of the Wild

  • Nier Automata

    Nier Automata is a story game about two androids that have to fight robots to save humanity. This is the most complex story that I have ever experienced. There are many themes and ideas Nier talks about. One that has stuck with me the most is how Nier covers Nihilism. Nier agrees that life is meaning but then will find meaning in the meaninglessness of life."Everything that lives is designed to end. They are perpetually trapped in a never-ending spiral of life and death. However, life is all about the struggle within this cycle.", Nier Automata.

  • Destiny 2

    Destiny 2 is a looter-shooter. I dumped roughly 1800 hours into this game because it is fun. I am addict to loot then shoot what I looted the loot again for something cooler. This aspect of looter-shooter combined with intense raids makes for a game that I am addicted to.

  • Halo 3

    Halo 3 is the first shooter I have ever played. The story of Halo is great and the pvp is also really good.

  • Tetris

    Tetris is the game I am best at. I have spent hundreds of hours practicing strategies and memorizing patterns. I used to be ranked 49th world wide until I stopped playing.

  • Legend of Zelda Breath of the Wild

    Legend of Zelda BotW is an experience like no other. The games physics and mechanics merge together so well and the player has so much freedom with the mechanics.
