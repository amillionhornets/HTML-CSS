3.0 out of 5 stars Stick with the single CD remastered version
Reviewed in the United States on January 29, 2019
Verified Purchase
This review is only for the remastered 3 CD version, focusing on whether or not you should buy this one. This album, in my opinion, is one of the greatest albums of all time and is easily a 5 star album. I assume that most here already know that and are looking to upgrade their old CD to a fresh new remaster.

I already have a 24 caret gold version of this album, not the original CD, so my comparison may not be the same as yours. The first CD of this is the original album remastered. Comparing the CDs with headphones the 2018 remaster is almost exactly the same as my gold CD. I can't recommend buying this if you happen to have the gold CD.

The 2nd CD is rough versions of the album songs, and these are very rough. I sometimes like to hear alternate versions of songs, but these are so rough I can't really see myself listening to them again. I think they actually take away from the greatness of the original tracks. The 3rd CD is a concert. Sounds like a bootleg recording. If this is the best they could find, they probably shouldn't have bothered. I'll give it a few more listens, but it's very low quality and really only good for an historical account.

The 3 CD version comes with an upgraded booklet which is nice and the package looks good.

It pains me to give this 3 stars as it's one of my favorite albums. If you need to upgrade your old CD, just buy the single CD version as it's at a great price.
